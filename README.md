This repository hosts the official, public-facing website for the YouthHelpYouth initiative, operating under the theme: We Get Up Stronger.
Our primary mission is to provide immediate, crucial support to youth and families in Western Jamaica (including Westmoreland, Hanover, and St. James) who have been affected by the devastating effects of Hurricane Melissa.

The website serves as the central hub for the following efforts:
7-Day Relief Bag Drive: Organizing and funding essential relief bags containing food, water, and hygiene items for families.
Donation Collection: Providing secure and transparent ways for the public to contribute financially to relief and reconstruction efforts.
Community Updates: Sharing real-time news, distribution points, and success stories to maintain transparency and engagement.
